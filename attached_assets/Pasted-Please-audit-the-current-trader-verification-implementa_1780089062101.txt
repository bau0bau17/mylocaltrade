Please audit the current trader verification implementation and extend it into a realistic business verification flow.

Important:
Do not rebuild existing trust badges or verification UI unnecessarily if already implemented correctly.
Reuse the current verified / unverified surfaces where possible.

Goal:
Create a practical trader vetting strategy that works not only for company owners, but also for authorised employees or managers who may represent a business without personally owning it or having access to every company document.

Requirements:

1. Verification levels
Support separate concepts for:
- account verification
- business verification
- verified trader approval

2. Trader role in business
Add a trader field such as:
- owner
- director
- manager
- employee
- self-employed
- other

Also add:
- authorised representative yes/no

3. Verification status
Add a structured verification status flow such as:
- not_started
- pending
- needs_more_info
- verified
- rejected

4. Business verification documents
Prepare support for trader verification documents such as:
- public liability insurance
- company registration proof
- VAT proof
- business address proof
- authorisation proof if the trader is not the owner
- optional business website or business email domain evidence

5. Non-owner strategy
Do not require every trader to be the business owner.
Allow employees or managers to represent a business if they can show they are authorised.

Examples of acceptable non-owner proof:
- business email domain
- manager/owner confirmation
- business document linking them to the company
- insurance or other business documents showing the company details

6. Badge logic
Keep verified badge separate from simple email/phone verification.
Do not show full verified trader approval unless admin review is complete.

7. Admin review
Add or improve admin review capability for trader verification:
- review submitted documents
- approve / reject / request more info
- add internal notes
- record who verified and when

8. Safety
Do not break:
- auth
- trader profiles
- subscriptions
- dashboard
- enquiries
- existing trust UI
- reviews
- messaging
- admin flows

9. Output
After the audit and changes, summarise:
- what verification logic already existed
- what was added
- what remains placeholder
- whether the app now supports non-owner authorised representatives properly
