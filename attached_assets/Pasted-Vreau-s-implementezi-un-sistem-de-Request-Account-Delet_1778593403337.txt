Vreau să implementezi un sistem de Request Account Deletion pentru MyLocalTrade, atât în aplicația mobilă, cât și în Admin Dashboard.

Important: nu vreau hard delete instant pentru toate datele. MyLocalTrade este o platformă unde clienții și traderii comunică direct, există verificări de traderi, documente, plăți, reviews, messages, complaints și audit logs. De aceea, account deletion trebuie făcut controlat.

Scopul este:

* utilizatorul poate cere ștergerea contului;
* accesul la cont este dezactivat;
* profilul public al traderului este ascuns imediat;
* datele care nu mai sunt necesare sunt șterse sau anonimizate;
* anumite date pot fi păstrate limitat pentru fraud prevention, disputes, accounting, legal obligations, platform safety sau legal claims;
* adminul poate vedea și procesa cererile de deletion;
* totul este auditat.

⸻

1. Mobile App — Account Settings

Adaugă în Account Settings o opțiune:

Delete Account

sau

Request Account Deletion

Când userul apasă, deschide un ecran separat cu explicație clară.

Text recomandat:

Deleting your account will remove access to your account and your profile will no longer be available. Some records may be retained for a limited period where required for fraud prevention, legal, accounting, dispute resolution or platform safety purposes.

Pentru traderi, adaugă text suplimentar:

Your public trader profile will be hidden immediately and you will no longer appear in search results. Verification, payment, audit, complaint and message records may be retained for a limited period where necessary.

⸻

2. Confirmation before deletion request

Înainte să trimită requestul, cere:

* password confirmation, dacă este disponibil;
* checkbox obligatoriu;
* optional deletion reason.

Checkbox text:

I understand that my account access will be disabled and some records may be retained for a limited period where legally necessary.

Button:

Submit Account Deletion Request

După submit, afișează:

Your account deletion request has been received. Your access may be disabled and your public profile, if any, will be removed or hidden. We may retain limited records where required for fraud prevention, disputes, legal, accounting or platform safety purposes.

⸻

3. Customer deletion behaviour

Când un customer cere ștergerea contului:

* set account_status = DELETION_REQUESTED;
* revoke active sessions/tokens;
* delete push notification tokens;
* stop marketing communications;
* disable future login, dacă deletion request este aprobat automat;
* remove/anonymise profile details where possible;
* remove saved traders/favourites;
* keep enquiry/message/review records only where necessary for disputes, fraud prevention, audit, complaints or legal reasons;
* if public reviews remain, anonymise display name to “Deleted user” unless there is a legal reason to retain identity internally.

⸻

4. Trader deletion behaviour

Când un trader cere ștergerea contului:

Immediately:

* hide public trader profile;
* remove trader from public search results;
* set trader profile visibility = HIDDEN or DELETION_REQUESTED;
* stop new enquiries;
* hide public portfolio/gallery images;
* revoke active sessions/tokens;
* delete push notification tokens;
* stop marketing communications;
* disable login after request is accepted or immediately if designed that way;
* mark subscription for cancellation or show admin action required depending on current billing logic.

Do not instantly hard-delete:

* identity verification records;
* uploaded ID/document records;
* insurance records;
* qualification records;
* Companies House check records;
* payment/subscription records;
* audit logs;
* complaints/reports;
* messages/enquiries;
* review moderation history.

These retained records must be restricted and must not be publicly visible.

7. Retention rules

Make retention periods configurable in one place.

Suggested defaults:

Customer profile data:

* anonymise within 30 days unless needed for disputes, fraud prevention or legal reasons.

Trader public profile:

* hide immediately.

Trader verification documents:

* retain for up to 24 months after account closure where necessary for fraud prevention, disputes, legal claims or platform safety.
* If there is an active complaint, dispute, investigation or legal issue, retention can be extended with a documented reason.

Payment/accounting records:

* retain for up to 6 years where required for accounting, tax or legal obligations.

Complaints, reports and audit logs:

* retain for up to 6 years where needed for platform safety, disputes, fraud prevention or legal claims.

Messages and enquiries:

* retain where needed for disputes, fraud prevention, platform safety or legal claims.
* otherwise anonymise personal identifiers after the retention period.

Do not retain personal data forever without a documented reason.

10. Automatic actions after deletion request

When a deletion request is submitted:

* create audit log entry ACCOUNT_DELETION_REQUESTED;
* send confirmation email to user;
* notify admin;
* if user is trader, immediately hide public profile;
* revoke sessions/tokens or mark account for access disable;
* remove push tokens;
* opt out from marketing;
* prevent new public enquiries if trader;
* prevent trader from appearing in public search;
* keep required retained records restricted.

For traders, public visibility removal must happen immediately, not after admin review.

⸻

If email fails, do not fail the API request. Log the email failure safely.

14. Reviews after account deletion

If a customer deletes their account:

* existing approved reviews may remain visible if needed for platform integrity;
* display name should become “Deleted user”;
* internal original user link can be retained only where necessary and restricted.

If a trader deletes their account:

* public profile should be hidden;
* reviews should not appear on a public trader profile if the profile is no longer public;
* internal review records can be retained for disputes, audit or legal reasons.

⸻

15. Messages and enquiries after deletion

If customer deletes account:

* customer cannot send new messages;
* existing conversation records may be retained where needed;
* display name can be anonymised to “Deleted user”.

If trader deletes account:

* trader cannot receive new enquiries;
* existing conversations should be closed or marked unavailable;
* customers should see a safe message if they open an old conversation:

This trader is no longer available on MyLocalTrade.

Do not expose deleted trader contact details publicly.

⸻

16. Subscription handling

If a trader requests account deletion and has an active subscription:

* mark subscription for cancellation or redirect to current cancellation flow;
* do not allow public visibility after deletion request;
* store subscription/payment records as required for accounting/legal purposes;
* do not hard-delete Stripe customer/subscription references immediately if needed for payment records.

If current Stripe logic requires manual handling, show admin alert:

This trader has an active subscription. Please review billing before completing deletion.

⸻

17. Security

Requirements:

* only logged-in user can request deletion for their own account;
* admin can view deletion requests;
* normal users cannot view deletion requests;
* trader cannot request deletion for another trader;
* customer cannot request deletion for another user;
* deletion endpoints must be rate limited;
* password confirmation should be required where possible;
* deletion request must be auditable;
* avoid logging sensitive personal data;
* do not expose retained private documents publicly.

⸻

18. UI wording

Use careful wording.

Do not say:

Your data will be deleted immediately
Everything will be permanently removed now
We will erase all records instantly

Use:

Your public profile will be hidden immediately where applicable. Some records may be retained for a limited period where required for fraud prevention, legal, accounting, dispute resolution or platform safety purposes.
